# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Graveyard Jokes Studios ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Hollow Press (hollowpress.graveyardjokes.com).

## 1. Information We Collect

When you visit this site, we automatically collect:

- IP address and approximate geographic location
- Browser type and version
- Visit timestamps and page views
- Referrer information

When you register an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Username and password (hashed)

When you use the contact form, we collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Message content

When you post a comment, we collect:

- Name or username
- Comment content

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the service
- Manage user accounts and authentication
- Moderate comments
- Respond to contact inquiries
- Understand site usage and improve user experience
- Ensure site security and prevent abuse

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **AWS S3** — for asset storage
- **hCaptcha** — for bot protection during registration
- **Zoho Mail** — for email notifications

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or as described in this policy.

## 4. Cookies

We use essential session cookies for authentication and CSRF protection. See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for details.

## 5. Comment Moderation

Comments submitted by users are held for moderation before being published. We store comment content and the submitter's name.

## 6. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

## 7. Your Rights

To access, correct, or request deletion of your data, contact us at [admin@graveyardjokes.com](mailto:admin@graveyardjokes.com).

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
