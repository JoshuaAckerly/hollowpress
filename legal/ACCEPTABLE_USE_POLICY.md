# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** July 6, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy governs your use of Hollow Press (hollowpress.graveyardjokes.com) operated by Graveyard Jokes Studios.

## 1. Prohibited Activities

You may not use this service to:

- Violate any applicable law
- Post or transmit illegal, harmful, harassing, or hateful content
- Transmit malware or malicious code
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the service or its systems
- Harass, threaten, or impersonate others
- Spam or send unsolicited bulk communications
- Infringe on intellectual property rights

## 2. Comment Standards

All comments must comply with applicable law, be respectful, and not contain spam, hate speech, or harmful content. Comments are subject to moderation.

## 3. Reporting Violations

Report violations to [admin@graveyardjokes.com](mailto:admin@graveyardjokes.com).

## 4. Enforcement

Violations may result in comment removal, account suspension, or legal action.
